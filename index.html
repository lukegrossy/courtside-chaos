DAY ONE

Well, would ya look at that — the new owner, in the flesh. Pleasure to meetcha. I'm Sandra. I keep the books, the schedule, and most of the chaos under control. Or at least I try to.

Quick lay of the land, then. Coach Willis is around here somewhere — if the men's room door is closed, that's him. Don't knock. Ricky's not speaking to the bus driver again, which works out, because the bus driver isn't speaking to Ricky either.

Tiny did forty minutes at the Open Mic downtown last night, and about thirty of 'em were an impression of you. I'd call it unflattering, but the crowd disagreed. Loudly.

The mascot's on the injured list. Don't ask. There's a folder.
